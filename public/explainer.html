Certificates

Gas Safety Certificates — official GSR format

The app generates Domestic/Landlord Gas Safety Records that mirror the official Gas Safe layout exactly: black section headers, yellow column headers, alternating grey rows.

Fill in up to 4 appliances with full inspection data — CO and CO2 readings, flue type, combustion analysis, safety devices, ventilation, flue visual and performance, and safe-to-use status.

The next inspection date is auto-calculated one year forward. Download as PDF or email directly from the app.

Records

Every job and document — stored and searchable

The Records screen organises everything into folders. Tap any folder to see all records inside, preview them, download PDFs, or email them to customers.

The Reminders Due folder automatically flags any property whose annual inspection falls within the next 30 days — with a pre-filled reminder email ready to send.

Records can be exported as a single file and imported on another device to stay in sync across phones and tablets.

Your yearly report — file your own tax return, no accountant needed

Export a CSV statement from your bank and upload it. The app matches income to your paid invoices and lets you categorise every expense — materials, vehicle, software, wages, rent, and more.

Save hundreds every year. Skip the paper certificate pads and skip the accountant — at £4.99/month the app pays for itself many times over.

Email import

Import certificates from email — no re-typing

Already issue certificates via your office software? Copy and paste the email text into the app. The parser automatically extracts the customer name, address, appliance details, combustion readings, and inspection results.

Works for five certificate types: Gas Safety Certificates, Boiler Service records, Gas Works, Gas Isolation certificates, and Warning Notices. Parsed records go straight into the correct Records folder.

Reminders

Never miss an annual inspection

The Reminders Due screen scans all Gas Safety Certificates and Boiler Service records and flags any property whose annual inspection falls within the next 30 days.

For each property due, the app generates a pre-filled reminder email — with the correct address, appliance details, and due date already filled in, ready to copy and send.

Important reminders can be pinned so they stay visible even after the 30-day window.

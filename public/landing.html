FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about the Gas Safety App.

What gas safety certificates can I create? +

Gas Safety App supports 11 certificate types: CP12 Landlord Gas Safety Records, Domestic Gas Safety Records, Boiler Service Records, Boiler Commissioning Checklists, Gas Warning Notices, LPG Safety Records, Commercial Gas Safety Records, Catering Gas Safety Records, Unvented Hot Water Records, Invoices, and Quotes. Every certificate includes digital signatures and PDF export.

Can I attach flue gas analyser readings? +

Yes. Every certificate type supports photographic evidence and PDF imports from gas analysers and manometers. You can photograph readings, import PDF print-outs from Bluetooth-connected analysers, and attach them directly within the certificate workflow — no more separate paperwork.

Does the app work offline? +

Yes. Gas Safety App is a Progressive Web App (PWA) that works offline. Create and save certificates without an internet connection — they’ll be available the moment you’re back online. Works on any device with a browser.

How much does it cost? +

Plans start at £7.50/month (Lite) with all 11 certificate types included. Pro is £14.50/month with unlimited certificates and accounts. Pro+ is £18.50/month with multi-engineer support. Annual billing gives you 2 months free. No setup fees, cancel anytime.

Is it compliant with UK gas safety regulations? +

Yes. All certificate templates follow the formats required under the Gas Safety (Installation and Use) Regulations 1998. The app is designed exclusively for Gas Safe registered engineers working in the UK.

What devices does it work on? +

Gas Safety App runs in any modern browser on phones, tablets, laptops, and desktops. As a PWA, you can install it to your home screen on iPhone, iPad, Android, Windows, or Mac — no app store required.
